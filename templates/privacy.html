Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Curated collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you browse our site, place an order, or contact our team.

Last updated: March 23, 2026

At a glance

  • We collect the details needed to process orders and support customers.
  • We use cookies and analytics to improve site performance and usability.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • You can contact us to review, update, or delete your information where applicable.

1. Information we collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, billing details, and any message you send through our contact forms or support channels.

We may also collect technical information including your browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, and referral sources.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • process orders and deliver products,
  • respond to questions and support requests,
  • send important service updates,
  • improve our website, catalog, and customer experience,
  • prevent fraud, misuse, and unauthorized activity, and
  • comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

3. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies, local storage, and similar tools to remember user preferences, maintain site functionality, and understand how visitors interact with our pages. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect some features.

4. Sharing information

We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, process payments, fulfill deliveries, or provide customer support. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their services.

We may also disclose information when required by law or when needed to protect our rights, users, or business operations.

5. Data retention and security

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, accounting, or support purposes. We use practical technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, but no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

6. Your choices and rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate data, or request deletion of information that is no longer required.

To make a privacy-related request, please contact us through our contact page or email the team directly.

7. Third-party links

Our website may include links to third-party websites or services. Once you leave our site, their privacy practices apply, and we are not responsible for the content or policies of those external services.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of the site after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
